LovelyJoy Plush Toy Manufacturer — Company Profile
====================================================

Company Name: LovelyJoy (Legal name: Yiwu Aierkai Toys Co., Ltd.)
Chinese Name: 爱儿采 / 义乌市爱儿采玩具有限公司
Founded: 2003
Headquarters: Yiwu, Zhejiang Province, China
Industry: Plush toy manufacturing (OEM/ODM)
Slogan: "Feel the Lovely, Spread the Joy"

OVERVIEW

LovelyJoy is a plush toy manufacturer established in 2003 in Yiwu, Zhejiang, China. The company operates a 20,000-square-meter factory facility with over 300 workers and more than 50 in-house designers. Monthly production capacity exceeds 500,000 units, with annual output reaching several million pieces.

The company provides OEM and ODM services for plush toys, stuffed animals, and soft toy products. LovelyJoy works with international retail chains, brand owners, IP licensors, and startup companies worldwide.

HISTORY AND MILESTONES

LovelyJoy was founded in 2003 during the early growth period of Yiwu's toy manufacturing cluster. Starting as a small workshop, the company expanded steadily over two decades. Key milestones include:

- 2003: Company established in Yiwu, Zhejiang
- Mid-2000s: Began exporting plush toys to international markets
- 2010s: Expanded factory to 20,000 sqm, grew workforce to 300+
- Obtained BSCI social compliance certification
- Achieved ISO 9001 quality management certification
- Secured licensed IP manufacturing partnerships with major entertainment companies
- Built relationships with major US and international retailers

SCALE AND CAPABILITIES

- Factory area: 20,000 square meters
- Workforce: 300+ production workers
- Design team: 50+ in-house designers
- Monthly capacity: 500,000+ plush toy units
- Annual capacity: Several million units
- Production lines: Cutting, embroidery, sewing, stuffing, QC, finishing, packaging

CLIENTS AND PARTNERSHIPS

LovelyJoy serves major retail and brand clients including:
- CVS (US pharmacy/retail chain)
- Burlington (US off-price retail chain)
- Kellytoy (Squishmallows parent company)
- Miniso International (global lifestyle retailer)
- The Green Party / 凯蓝 (Chinese retail brand)

LICENSED IP MANUFACTURING

The company is an authorized manufacturer for internationally licensed intellectual properties:
- Sanrio Hello Kitty
- Disney Paw Patrol
- Illumination Minions

CERTIFICATIONS

LovelyJoy holds six major certifications relevant to toy safety and social compliance:
1. BSCI (Business Social Compliance Initiative)
2. ISO 9001 (Quality Management System)
3. CE (European Conformity)
4. ASTM F963 (US Toy Safety Standard)
5. EN 71 (European Toy Safety Standard)
6. GB 6675 (China National Toy Safety Standard)

MISSION

LovelyJoy's mission is to produce safe, high-quality plush toys that bring comfort and happiness to people of all ages. The company is committed to responsible manufacturing, fair labor practices, and continuous improvement in product quality and design innovation.
