LovelyJoy — Product Categories and Specifications
====================================================

OVERVIEW

LovelyJoy manufactures a wide range of plush toy products. All categories are available for OEM (client-designed) and ODM (LovelyJoy-designed) production. This document describes each product category with typical specifications.

CATEGORY 1: STUFFED ANIMALS

Description: Classic plush animals including bears, dogs, cats, rabbits, elephants, lions, pandas, unicorns, dinosaurs, and any other animal species.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 15cm to 100cm+
- Materials: Short plush, crystal super-soft, long plush
- Filling: PP cotton, PE pellets for weighted/sitting versions
- Features: Embroidered or plastic safety eyes, embroidered nose and mouth
- Styles: Realistic, cartoon/kawaii, chibi, minimalist

Popular sub-types:
- Teddy bears (classic, jointed, dressed)
- Farm animals (cow, pig, sheep, horse, chicken)
- Wild animals (lion, tiger, giraffe, elephant, monkey)
- Ocean animals (whale, dolphin, shark, octopus, turtle)
- Fantasy animals (unicorn, dragon, phoenix)

CATEGORY 2: CHARACTER AND IP PLUSH

Description: Plush toys based on licensed intellectual properties or original character designs.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 15cm to 60cm
- Materials: Crystal super-soft or short plush (per IP style guide)
- Features: Precise color matching to brand guidelines, detailed embroidery
- Compliance: Licensed holographic stickers, brand-approved packaging
- IP experience: Sanrio Hello Kitty, Disney Paw Patrol, Illumination Minions

CATEGORY 3: PLUSH KEYCHAINS AND ACCESSORIES

Description: Small plush items designed as keychains, bag charms, phone charms, or clip-ons.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 8cm to 15cm
- Materials: Short plush, crystal super-soft
- Attachments: Metal keychain ring, lobster clasp, carabiner clip, suction cup
- Features: Simplified construction for small scale, embroidered details
- Packaging: Individual poly bag or header card display

CATEGORY 4: PLUSH PILLOWS AND CUSHIONS

Description: Decorative and functional plush pillows shaped as animals, food, objects, or characters.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 30cm to 80cm
- Materials: Crystal super-soft, minky, velboa
- Filling: PP cotton (fluffy) or memory foam (supportive)
- Styles: Round, flat, 3D sculptural, body pillow
- Features: Printed or embroidered faces, removable covers (optional)

CATEGORY 5: WEIGHTED PLUSH AND COMFORT TOYS

Description: Plush toys with added weight for sensory comfort and anxiety relief. Growing market category.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 25cm to 60cm
- Weight: 0.5kg to 3kg (glass beads or PE pellets in inner bag)
- Materials: Crystal super-soft, minky (extra soft for comfort use)
- Features: Weighted inner pouch sewn inside, lavender scent option
- Safety: Inner bag securely stitched, double-bagged pellets for child safety

CATEGORY 6: PLUSH BACKPACKS AND BAGS

Description: Functional bags designed as plush animal or character shapes.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 25cm to 45cm
- Materials: Short plush or crystal super-soft exterior, polyester lining
- Features: Zipper closure, adjustable straps, internal pockets
- Styles: Animal-shaped backpacks, crossbody plush bags, plush coin purses

CATEGORY 7: INTERACTIVE AND ELECTRONIC PLUSH

Description: Plush toys with electronic features including sound, light, movement, or recording.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 20cm to 50cm
- Electronics: Sound module (pre-recorded or custom), LED lights, vibration motor
- Power: Button cell batteries (replaceable), USB rechargeable (selected models)
- Features: Squeeze-to-activate, pull-string, push-button activation
- Compliance: Additional electrical toy safety testing required

CATEGORY 8: BABY AND INFANT PLUSH

Description: Plush toys specifically designed for babies and infants (ages 0-3).

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 15cm to 30cm
- Materials: Organic cotton, certified-safe plush, hypoallergenic materials
- Safety: Embroidered eyes only (no plastic parts), no detachable accessories, no ribbons longer than 15cm
- Features: Rattle inserts, teething rings (BPA-free), crinkle elements
- Compliance: Strictest tier of ASTM F963/EN 71 testing
- Packaging: Clearly labeled for age 0+

CATEGORY 9: SEASONAL AND HOLIDAY PLUSH

Description: Plush toys designed for specific holidays and seasonal promotions.

Categories:
- Valentine's Day: Hearts, bears with roses, love-themed animals
- Easter: Bunnies, chicks, lambs, eggs
- Halloween: Pumpkins, ghosts, bats, black cats, witches
- Christmas: Santa, reindeer, snowmen, elves, penguins
- Chinese New Year: Zodiac animals, lucky cats

Typical order characteristics:
- Higher volumes per SKU due to seasonal demand
- Strict delivery deadlines aligned with retail selling windows
- Often repeated annually with updated designs

CATEGORY 10: PROMOTIONAL AND CORPORATE PLUSH

Description: Custom plush toys for brand promotion, corporate gifts, event giveaways, and mascot merchandise.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 15cm to 30cm (compact for giveaway/shipping)
- Features: Custom branded clothing (T-shirts, caps, scarves with company logo)
- Customization: Company mascot designs, brand color matching, logo embroidery
- Packaging: Custom boxes or bags with corporate branding
- MOQ: Often lower volume with higher customization

CATEGORY 11: GIANT AND DISPLAY PLUSH

Description: Oversized plush toys for retail display, photo opportunities, or premium gifts.

Typical specifications:
- Sizes: 100cm to 200cm
- Materials: Long plush or crystal super-soft
- Features: Reinforced internal structure for shape retention
- Filling: High-density PP cotton, sometimes with internal frame
- Applications: Store window displays, trade show attractions, premium gifts

CUSTOM SPECIFICATIONS

Every product in every category can be customized. LovelyJoy produces to your exact specifications:
- Custom sizes to the centimeter
- Custom Pantone-matched colors
- Custom materials and fillings
- Custom features and accessories
- Custom packaging and branding

LovelyJoy — 产品品类与规格
====================================================

概述

爱儿采生产多种类型的毛绒玩具产品，所有品类均支持OEM和ODM。

品类1：毛绒动物
经典动物造型：熊、狗、猫、兔、象等。尺寸15-100cm+，多种面料和风格。

品类2：角色/IP毛绒
授权IP或原创角色玩具。精确配色和品牌合规。经验：Hello Kitty、汪汪队、小黄人。

品类3：毛绒钥匙扣与配饰
8-15cm小型毛绒，配钥匙环、龙虾扣、吸盘等。

品类4：毛绒抱枕
动物、食物、物品造型抱枕。30-80cm，PP棉或记忆棉填充。

品类5：加重毛绒/安抚玩具
含重力颗粒的感觉统合安抚玩具。0.5-3kg，玻璃珠或PE颗粒。

品类6：毛绒背包与包袋
动物或角色造型功能包袋，拉链、可调节肩带。

品类7：互动电子毛绒
含发声、发光、震动等电子功能。需额外电子玩具安全测试。

品类8：婴幼儿毛绒（0-3岁）
仅绣花眼、无可拆卸部件、无长丝带，有机棉/低敏材料，最严格安全测试。

品类9：节日季节性毛绒
情人节、复活节、万圣节、圣诞节、春节等主题产品。

品类10：促销/企业定制毛绒
品牌吉祥物、企业礼品、活动赠品。定制品牌服装和Logo。

品类11：巨型展示毛绒
100-200cm超大尺寸，内部加固结构，适合陈列和高端礼品。

所有品类均可按客户精确规格定制。
