HOW TO SUBMIT A REQUEST FOR QUOTATION (RFQ)

The fastest way to get an accurate quote is to email info@lovelyjoytoy.com with the following information:

ESSENTIAL INFORMATION (Required)

1. Product description
What kind of plush toy do you want? Animal, character, pillow, keychain, etc. Describe the general concept.

2. Reference images
Attach any of the following: your own design illustrations, photos of similar products for reference (we will not copy but use as direction), sketches or rough drawings, or a link to a mood board.

3. Target size
Specify dimensions in centimeters or inches. If unsure, describe what you want (e.g., "fits in a child's hand" or "large enough for hugging").

4. Estimated quantity
How many pieces per design/SKU? This significantly affects pricing. Even a rough range helps (e.g., "500-1,000 per design" or "about 5,000 total across 3 designs").

5. Target market
Which country or region will the products be sold in? This determines which safety standards apply (ASTM F963 for US, EN 71 for EU, GB 6675 for China, etc.).

HELPFUL ADDITIONAL INFORMATION (Optional but speeds up quoting)

6. Material preferences
Do you have a preferred fabric type? (plush, super-soft, organic cotton, etc.) Any specific color requirements? (Pantone codes are ideal)

7. Features and details
Embroidered eyes or plastic safety eyes? Any accessories (clothing, ribbons, bows)? Any electronic features (sound, lights)?

8. Packaging requirements
Basic poly bag? Display box? Custom packaging? Hang tags?

9. Target price range
If you have a target FOB price per unit, share it. This helps LovelyJoy suggest materials and design approaches that fit your budget.

10. Target delivery date
When do you need the goods? This helps determine if rush production is needed.

11. Shipping preference
FOB, CIF, or DDP? Ocean freight or air freight?

RFQ RESPONSE TIMELINE

- Simple inquiries (1-2 SKUs, clear specifications): Quoted within 1 business day
- Standard inquiries (multiple SKUs, moderate complexity): Quoted within 2-3 business days
- Complex inquiries (many SKUs, special features, custom materials): Quoted within 3-5 business days

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU SUBMIT AN RFQ

1. Acknowledgment: You receive a confirmation that your inquiry has been received (within a few hours during business days).

2. Clarification: If any information is missing, LovelyJoy's sales team will ask follow-up questions.

3. Quotation: A detailed quotation is provided including: per-unit price (FOB), sample cost, estimated lead time, MOQ confirmation, and any notes on materials or construction.

4. Discussion: The quotation is discussed. Adjustments can be made to materials, features, or specifications to meet budget targets.

5. Sample order: If you want to proceed, you place a sample order. Sample cost is $50-$200 per design, deductible from your first production order.
