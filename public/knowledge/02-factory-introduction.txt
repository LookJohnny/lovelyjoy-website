LovelyJoy Plush Toy Factory — Facility and Production Overview
===============================================================

FACTORY LOCATION

LovelyJoy's manufacturing facility is located at No.8 Siyuan Road, Niansan Li, Yiwu, Zhejiang Province, China. Yiwu is one of the world's largest small commodity trading hubs and a major center for toy manufacturing in China.

FACILITY SPECIFICATIONS

- Total factory area: 20,000 square meters (approximately 215,000 square feet)
- Building: Multi-story industrial facility
- Layout: Dedicated zones for cutting, embroidery, sewing, stuffing, quality control, finishing, and packaging
- Warehouse: On-site raw material and finished goods storage
- Design studio: In-house design center with 3D modeling and pattern-making capabilities
- Sample room: Dedicated prototyping and sample development workshop
- Showroom: Separate showroom at 4F-84556, Gate 188, Global Digital Trade Center, Yiwu

PRODUCTION LINES AND EQUIPMENT

The factory operates multiple parallel production lines equipped with:

Cutting Department:
- Computerized fabric cutting machines for precision and waste reduction
- Manual cutting stations for specialty fabrics and small-batch orders
- Pattern digitization and grading systems

Embroidery Department:
- Multi-head computerized embroidery machines
- Capacity for detailed facial features, logos, and decorative stitching
- Support for both flat and 3D embroidery techniques

Sewing Department:
- Industrial sewing machines (single needle, double needle, overlock)
- Specialized machines for different stitch types and fabric weights
- Multiple parallel sewing lines to handle concurrent orders

Stuffing Department:
- Automated and semi-automated stuffing machines
- Precision filling for consistent weight and firmness
- Support for various fill materials: PP cotton, PE pellets, memory foam, organic fillings

Finishing Department:
- Hand-finishing stations for detail work
- Brush and trim stations for surface quality
- Metal detector screening for safety compliance

Packaging Department:
- Poly-bagging machines with suffocation warning labels
- Custom box assembly and carton packing stations
- Barcode and label application equipment

WORKFORCE

- Total production workers: 300+
- Design team: 50+ in-house designers and pattern makers
- Quality control team: Dedicated QC inspectors at each production stage
- Management and support staff: Administrative, logistics, and customer service teams

PRODUCTION CAPACITY

- Monthly output: 500,000+ plush toy units
- Annual output: Several million units
- Ability to run multiple product lines simultaneously
- Flexible capacity allocation between large orders and smaller runs

OPERATING STANDARDS

The factory operates under the following management and compliance frameworks:
- ISO 9001 quality management system
- BSCI social compliance (labor conditions, worker safety, fair wages)
- 5S workplace organization methodology
- Regular third-party audits and inspections

VISITOR INFORMATION

Factory visits and audits are welcome with advance scheduling. Contact info@lovelyjoytoy.com to arrange a visit. The Yiwu showroom is open for walk-in visits during business hours.

LovelyJoy 毛绒玩具工厂 — 设施与生产概览
===============================================================

工厂位置

爱儿采生产基地位于中国浙江省义乌市廿三里四园路8号。义乌是全球最大的小商品集散中心之一，也是中国主要的玩具生产基地。

设施规格

- 工厂总面积：20,000平方米
- 布局：设有裁剪、绣花、车缝、充棉、质检、整形、包装等专用区域
- 仓库：厂内设有原材料和成品仓库
- 设计中心：配备3D建模和打版能力的内部设计工作室
- 样品室：专用打样和样品开发车间
- 展厅：义乌国际数字贸易中心188号门4楼84556

生产线与设备

裁剪车间：
- 电脑裁床，精准裁切，减少浪费
- 手工裁剪工位，适用于特殊面料和小批量订单
- 数字化打版和放码系统

绣花车间：
- 多头电脑绣花机
- 支持精细面部特征、Logo和装饰刺绣
- 平绣和立体绣花工艺

车缝车间：
- 工业缝纫机（单针、双针、包缝）
- 专用设备适应不同针法和面料厚度
- 多条平行生产线，可同时处理多个订单

充棉车间：
- 自动和半自动充棉机
- 精确填充，保证重量和手感一致性
- 支持多种填充材料：PP棉、PE颗粒、记忆棉、有机填充物

整形车间：
- 手工整形工位
- 梳毛和修剪工位
- 金属探测检测，确保安全合规

包装车间：
- 自动装袋机（含窒息警告标签）
- 定制礼盒和纸箱包装工位
- 条码和标签贴附设备

员工团队

- 生产工人：300余人
- 设计团队：50余名设计师和打版师
- 品控团队：各工序配备专职质检员
- 管理及支持团队：行政、物流、客服人员

产能

- 月产量：50万件以上
- 年产量：数百万件
- 可同时运行多条产品线
- 灵活调配产能，兼顾大单和小批量订单

运营标准

- ISO 9001 质量管理体系
- BSCI 社会合规（劳动条件、工人安全、公平薪酬）
- 5S 现场管理
- 定期接受第三方审核和验厂

参观信息

欢迎预约参观工厂和验厂，请联系 info@lovelyjoytoy.com 安排行程。义乌展厅在工作时间内接受自由参观。
