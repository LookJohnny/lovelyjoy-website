LovelyJoy — Packaging Solutions for Plush Toys
================================================

OVERVIEW

Packaging serves multiple functions: protecting the product during shipping, presenting the product attractively at retail, communicating brand identity, and meeting regulatory labeling requirements. LovelyJoy offers a full range of packaging solutions from basic poly bags to premium gift boxes.

STANDARD PACKAGING OPTIONS

1. Poly Bag (OPP Bag)
- Clear plastic bag, heat-sealed or self-adhesive closure
- Most economical option
- Required suffocation warning printed on bag (for bags larger than a certain size per US/EU regulations)
- Suitable for: bulk/wholesale, online orders, filler items
- Cost: Minimal addition to unit price

2. Poly Bag with Header Card
- Clear bag with a printed cardboard header card stapled or sealed at the top
- Header card displays brand logo, product name, UPC barcode, and age warning
- Peg-hole for retail hanging display
- Suitable for: convenience stores, gift shops, impulse purchase displays
- Cost: Low addition ($0.05-$0.15 per unit depending on design)

3. Display Box (Window Box)
- Printed cardboard box with a clear plastic window showing the plush toy inside
- Full-color printing on all sides
- Displays brand, product info, safety warnings, UPC code
- Suitable for: retail shelf display, gift items, premium products
- Cost: $0.30-$1.50 per unit depending on size and print complexity

4. Closed Gift Box
- Fully enclosed printed box, often with magnetic closure or ribbon
- Premium presentation for high-end products
- Suitable for: gift market, collector items, licensed IP products
- Cost: $0.80-$3.00+ per unit depending on materials and finishing

5. Carry Bag / Tote
- Branded plastic or paper bag with handles
- Product visible through bag
- Suitable for: tourist/souvenir shops, theme park merchandise
- Cost: $0.20-$0.60 per unit

LABELING AND TAGS

Hang Tags:
- Custom-printed cardboard tags attached with string, ribbon, or plastic fastener
- Content: Brand logo, product name, price, barcode, care instructions, origin
- Standard size: 5x7 cm or custom sizes
- Finishes: Matte, gloss, spot UV, foil stamping
- Cost: $0.03-$0.10 per tag

Woven Labels:
- Fabric labels sewn into the toy's seam during production
- Content: Brand name/logo
- Standard placement: Bottom seam or side seam
- High perceived quality, permanent branding element
- Cost: $0.02-$0.05 per label

Printed Satin Care Labels:
- Soft satin labels with printed care and compliance information
- Content: Material composition, washing instructions, safety warnings, manufacturer info, country of origin
- Required by law in most markets (US FTC, EU regulations)
- Content must comply with target market regulations
- Cost: $0.02-$0.05 per label

UPC/EAN Barcode Labels:
- Adhesive labels with scannable barcode
- Applied to packaging or included on hang tags
- Client provides barcode numbers; LovelyJoy produces the labels
- Cost: $0.01-$0.02 per label

REGULATORY LABELING REQUIREMENTS

United States:
- Country of origin ("Made in China")
- Fiber content (per FTC Textile Act)
- Care instructions (per FTC Care Labeling Rule)
- Age grading and choking hazard warning (per CPSIA)
- Tracking label (manufacturer, production date, batch)
- CPSIA compliance mark
- Suffocation warning on poly bags

European Union:
- CE marking
- Importer name and address (required for EU market)
- Country of origin
- Age warning (if applicable)
- EN 71 compliance reference
- Care symbols (ISO 3758)

CUSTOM PACKAGING DESIGN SERVICE

LovelyJoy's design team can create custom packaging at no additional design fee for orders meeting MOQ:
- Box structural design and dieline creation
- Graphic design for box artwork
- Hang tag design
- Label content layout and compliance review

PACKAGING FOR E-COMMERCE

For clients selling through online channels, LovelyJoy offers:
- Mailer-friendly packaging (fits standard shipping boxes)
- Unboxing experience design (tissue paper, thank-you cards, stickers)
- Frustration-free packaging (easy open, minimal waste)
- Poly mailer bags with custom printing

INNER PACKAGING (SHIPPING PROTECTION)

- Individual poly bags protect each toy from moisture and dirt
- Tissue paper wrapping for premium products
- Egg crate dividers for boxed items to prevent crushing
- Silica gel packets for moisture control during ocean freight

EXPORT CARTON SPECIFICATIONS

- Standard export cartons: Double-wall corrugated cardboard
- Carton markings: Shipping marks, quantity, gross/net weight, carton dimensions, "Made in China"
- Packing list inserted in each carton
- Carton dimensions optimized for container loading efficiency
- Typical packing: 24-100+ pieces per carton depending on toy size
