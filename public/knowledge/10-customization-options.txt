LovelyJoy — Plush Toy Customization Options
=============================================

OVERVIEW

LovelyJoy offers comprehensive customization capabilities for plush toys. Every aspect of a plush toy can be tailored to your brand, design vision, and market requirements. This document outlines all available customization options.

SIZE CUSTOMIZATION

LovelyJoy produces plush toys in virtually any size:
- Keychain/clip size: 8-12 cm
- Small: 15-20 cm
- Medium: 25-35 cm
- Large: 40-60 cm
- Extra large: 70-100 cm
- Giant/display: 100-200 cm

Custom sizes to your exact specifications are standard practice. Sizes are measured from head to toe (sitting height for sitting poses) or total length for lying poses.

COLOR CUSTOMIZATION

- Standard colors: Choose from thousands of in-stock fabric colors across plush, velboa, minky, and other fabric types
- Pantone matching: Custom-dyed fabrics matched to your exact Pantone code (minimum fabric order applies, typically 300-500 meters per color)
- Multi-color designs: No limit on the number of fabric colors per design
- Color-matching service: Send a physical sample or detailed color reference for precise matching

SHAPE AND DESIGN

- Animals: Bears, dogs, cats, rabbits, elephants, unicorns, dinosaurs, and any other animal
- Characters: Custom characters, mascots, IP-licensed figures
- Food and objects: Pizza, avocado, donut, star, moon — any shape is possible
- Anatomically accurate: Realistic proportions for collector/educational toys
- Stylized: Kawaii, chibi, cartoon proportions
- Articulated: Poseable limbs with internal wire frames
- Flat: Pillow-style flat plush toys

EMBROIDERY OPTIONS

- Machine embroidery: Precise, consistent results for facial features, logos, and text
- Density options: Light fill, heavy fill, satin stitch, running stitch
- Thread colors: Hundreds of thread color options
- 3D embroidery: Raised/padded embroidery for dimensional details
- Maximum embroidery area depends on the toy's surface area and placement
- Embroidery digitization: LovelyJoy converts your artwork into embroidery files at no extra charge

PRINTING OPTIONS

- Heat transfer printing: For detailed graphics, photographic images, all-over patterns
- Sublimation printing: For vibrant, full-color designs on polyester fabrics
- Screen printing: For simple, bold designs on flat fabric panels before assembly
- DTG (Direct to Garment): For small-batch custom prints on cotton elements

ACCESSORIES AND ADD-ONS

Clothing and wearables:
- T-shirts, dresses, hoodies, capes, scarves, hats
- Velcro or snap closures for removable clothing
- Miniature shoes, boots, or sandals

Attachments:
- Ribbons and bows
- Backpacks and bags
- Glasses and hats
- Keychains and carabiner clips
- Suction cups for window display
- Magnets for refrigerator attachment

Functional features:
- Sound modules: Record custom messages, music, or sound effects
- LED lights: Glowing elements in ears, nose, or body
- Vibration motors: For massage or interactive features
- Warming/cooling elements: Microwaveable inserts, gel packs
- Scented inserts: Lavender, vanilla, or custom scent sachets
- Music boxes: Wind-up or pull-string mechanisms

PACKAGING CUSTOMIZATION

- Poly bags with custom header cards
- Custom printed display boxes with window
- Hang tags with your brand, care instructions, and UPC
- Woven labels and printed satin labels
- Tissue paper wrapping
- Gift boxes and premium packaging
- Retail-ready shelf packaging

BRANDING ELEMENTS

- Woven brand label sewn into seam
- Printed satin care label with washing instructions
- Hang tag with brand story and product info
- Custom embroidered or printed logo on the toy
- Branded packaging (boxes, bags, tissue paper)
- UPC/EAN barcode labels

SAFETY CUSTOMIZATIONS BY MARKET

- Under-3 age group: Embroidered features only (no plastic eyes/noses), no small detachable parts, no long ribbons
- US market: CPSIA-compliant labels, tracking labels, age grading warnings
- EU market: CE mark, EN 71 compliance labels, importer information
- All markets: Non-toxic materials, flame-retardant options, metal-free construction

MINIMUM QUANTITIES FOR CUSTOMIZATION

Most customization options are available at the standard MOQ of 500 pieces. Some options that require higher minimums:
- Custom-dyed fabric colors: Typically 300-500 meter minimum per color
- Custom sound modules: 500+ piece minimum
- Custom packaging boxes: 500+ piece minimum

HOW TO REQUEST CUSTOMIZATION

Provide as much detail as possible in your inquiry:
1. Reference images or sketches
2. Target size and any specific dimensions
3. Preferred materials and colors (Pantone codes if available)
4. Desired features and accessories
5. Packaging requirements
6. Target market (for safety compliance)
7. Quantity estimate

LovelyJoy — 毛绒玩具定制选项
=============================================

概述

爱儿采提供毛绒玩具全方位定制服务，从尺寸、颜色到功能、包装均可按需定制。

尺寸定制

钥匙扣款8-12cm、小型15-20cm、中型25-35cm、大型40-60cm、超大型70-100cm、巨型100-200cm。可按精确尺寸定制。

颜色定制

上千种现货面料色可选；潘通色号精确配色（需最低面料订量300-500米/色）；多色设计无限制。

造型设计

动物、角色、食物、物品、写实风、卡通风、Q版、可动关节、枕头款等各种造型。

绣花选项

机绣面部特征、Logo、文字，支持3D立体绣花，数百种线色可选。绣花文件免费制作。

印花选项

热转印、热升华、丝网印、数码直喷。

配件与附加功能

服装：T恤、裙子、帽子、围巾等，可拆卸
附件：丝带、钥匙扣、吸盘、磁铁
功能：发声模块、LED灯、振动、可加热/制冷、香薰内芯、音乐盒

包装定制

胶袋+卡头、开窗展示盒、吊牌、织标、缎标、薄纸包装、礼品盒、零售货架包装。

品牌元素

织标、缎面洗涤标、吊牌、绣花/印花Logo、品牌包装、UPC/EAN条码。

安全定制

3岁以下：仅用绣花特征，无小部件
美国市场：CPSIA合规标签
欧盟市场：CE标识、EN 71标签

大部分定制选项适用于标准MOQ 500件。定染面料、定制发声模块、定制包装盒通常需500件以上起订。
