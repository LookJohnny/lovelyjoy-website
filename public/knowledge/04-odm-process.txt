LovelyJoy — ODM Plush Toy Design and Manufacturing Process
=============================================================

WHAT IS ODM?

ODM (Original Design Manufacturing) means LovelyJoy handles both the design and manufacturing of plush toys. The client provides a concept, brief, or idea, and LovelyJoy's in-house design team creates the product from scratch. This is ideal for clients who do not have their own design capabilities.

WHO USES ODM SERVICE?

- Startup brands launching their first plush toy line
- Retailers wanting exclusive private-label products
- Companies entering the plush toy market for the first time
- Brands looking to expand their product range without hiring designers
- Promotional product companies needing custom mascots or gift items
- Influencers and content creators developing branded merchandise

LOVELYJOY'S DESIGN CAPABILITIES

- 50+ in-house designers specializing in plush toy design
- 2D concept illustration and character design
- 3D modeling and digital rendering
- Pattern making and engineering
- Trend research and market analysis
- Licensed IP experience (Sanrio, Disney, Illumination)
- Free design service included for orders meeting MOQ requirements

STEP-BY-STEP ODM PROCESS

Step 1: Creative Brief and Consultation (1-3 days)
Client shares their concept, which can be: a rough sketch, a mood board, a character description in words, a reference image (not to be copied, for inspiration only), target market information, intended retail price point, or any visual direction. LovelyJoy's design team schedules a consultation to clarify the vision.

Step 2: Concept Design and Illustration (5-10 days)
LovelyJoy's designers create 2-3 concept illustrations based on the brief. These include front, side, and back views of the proposed plush toy, along with color palette, size reference, and material suggestions. The client reviews and selects a direction. Revisions to the concept are included at no extra charge.

Step 3: Technical Design and Specification (3-5 days)
Once the concept is approved, the design team creates a full technical specification including: detailed construction drawings, pattern layout, material specifications (fabric type, color codes, fill type), embroidery or print artwork files, size chart (if multiple sizes), safety feature specifications, and packaging design (if requested).

Step 4: Sample Development (7-15 days)
A physical sample is produced based on the approved technical specification. First sample typically takes 7-15 days. Sample cost is $50-$200 depending on complexity and is deducted from the first production order.

Step 5: Sample Review and Refinement
The sample is shipped to the client. LovelyJoy provides 1-2 rounds of free revisions. Common adjustments include: size fine-tuning, color matching corrections, facial expression refinements, stuffing density adjustments, and material hand-feel optimization.

Step 6: Final Approval and Order Placement
Client approves the final sample. Production order is confirmed with quantities, delivery timeline, and payment terms. A 30% deposit is required to begin production.

Step 7: Production (30-45 days)
Mass production follows the same rigorous process as OEM orders: cutting, embroidery, sewing, stuffing, QC inspection, finishing, and packaging. Rush production available in 20 days with surcharge.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

For ODM projects, IP ownership terms are agreed upon before production begins. Typical arrangements:
- Client owns the design exclusively after paying for development
- LovelyJoy retains no rights to reproduce the design for other clients
- NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) available upon request
- Design files are handed over to client upon request after first order

PRICING

ODM pricing includes the design service cost, which is waived for orders meeting MOQ requirements (500+ pieces). For orders below MOQ, a design fee may apply depending on complexity. The per-unit manufacturing cost is quoted based on size, materials, complexity, and order quantity.

LovelyJoy — ODM 毛绒玩具设计与制造流程
=============================================================

什么是ODM？

ODM（原始设计制造）是指爱儿采同时负责毛绒玩具的设计和生产。客户提供概念、创意或想法，爱儿采的设计团队从零开始创作产品。适合没有自有设计能力的客户。

ODM适用客户

- 首次推出毛绒玩具产品线的初创品牌
- 需要独家自有品牌产品的零售商
- 首次进入毛绒玩具市场的企业
- 希望在不雇佣设计师的情况下扩展产品线的品牌
- 需要定制吉祥物或礼品的促销品公司
- 开发品牌周边的网红和内容创作者

爱儿采设计能力

- 50余名专业毛绒玩具设计师
- 2D概念插画和角色设计
- 3D建模和数字渲染
- 打版和工程设计
- 趋势研究和市场分析
- IP授权产品设计经验（三丽鸥、迪士尼、照明娱乐）
- 达到MOQ的订单免费提供设计服务

ODM流程详解

第一步：创意沟通（1-3天）
客户分享创意概念，可以是：草图、情绪板、文字描述、参考图片（仅作灵感参考）、目标市场信息、预期零售价位等。设计团队安排沟通会议明确方向。

第二步：概念设计与插画（5-10天）
设计师根据需求创作2-3个概念方案，包含正面、侧面、背面视图，配色方案、尺寸参考和材料建议。客户审阅并选定方向，概念阶段修改不额外收费。

第三步：技术设计与规格（3-5天）
概念确认后，设计团队制作完整技术规格书：详细结构图、版型布局、材料规格（面料类型、色号、填充类型）、绣花或印花文件、尺码表（如有多尺寸）、安全特征规格、包装设计（如需要）。

第四步：打样（7-15天）
根据技术规格制作实物样品。首次打样约7-15天。打样费$50-$200，视复杂度而定，首单可抵扣。

第五步：样品评审与修改
样品寄送客户。提供1-2次免费修改。常见调整：尺寸微调、颜色校正、表情细化、填充密度调整、手感优化。

第六步：终审确认与下单
客户确认最终样品，确定订单数量、交期和付款条件。需支付30%定金启动生产。

第七步：量产（30-45天）
量产流程与OEM相同：裁剪→绣花→车缝→充棉→质检→整形→包装。可加急20天完成，需加收费用。

知识产权

ODM项目的IP归属在生产前协商确定。通常安排：
- 客户支付开发费用后拥有设计独家所有权
- 爱儿采不保留为其他客户复制该设计的权利
- 可根据要求签署保密协议（NDA）
- 首单完成后可应客户要求移交设计文件

定价

ODM定价包含设计服务费，达到MOQ（500件以上）的订单免收设计费。低于MOQ的订单可能需要支付设计费，视复杂度而定。单件制造成本根据尺寸、材料、复杂度和订单数量报价。
