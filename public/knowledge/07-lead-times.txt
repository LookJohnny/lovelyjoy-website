LovelyJoy — Plush Toy Production Lead Times and Timelines
============================================================

OVERVIEW

Understanding lead times is essential for planning product launches, seasonal campaigns, and retail delivery windows. This document details every phase from initial inquiry to goods arriving at your warehouse.

PHASE-BY-PHASE TIMELINE

Phase 1: Inquiry and Quotation — 1-3 business days
After receiving your inquiry with product details, LovelyJoy provides a detailed quotation. Simple products are quoted within 1 day; complex or multi-SKU inquiries may take 2-3 days.

Phase 2: Design and Concept (ODM only) — 5-10 business days
If LovelyJoy is designing the product (ODM service), concept illustrations and technical design take 5-10 days. This phase is skipped for OEM orders where the client provides finished designs.

Phase 3: Sample Development — 7-15 business days
Physical sample production takes 7-15 days depending on complexity. Simple designs (basic shapes, few details) are closer to 7 days. Complex designs (multiple materials, accessories, large size) may take up to 15 days.

Phase 4: Sample Shipping — 4-7 business days
International express courier (DHL, FedEx, UPS) to most destinations. USA and Europe typically receive samples within 4-7 business days.

Phase 5: Client Review and Approval — Variable (client-dependent)
This is the phase clients should plan carefully, as it is entirely in their control. Allow at least 3-5 business days for internal review. If revisions are needed, each round adds 5-10 days for revised sample production plus 4-7 days for shipping.

Phase 6: Material Procurement — 5-10 business days
After order confirmation and deposit payment, raw materials are sourced. Standard fabrics and trims: 5-7 days. Custom-dyed fabrics, specialty materials, or imported components: 10-15 days.

Phase 7: Pre-Production Sample (PP Sample) — 3-5 business days
A final confirmation sample using actual production materials. Photos or video are shared for quick client approval. Physical PP sample can be shipped if the client requires it.

Phase 8: Mass Production — 30-45 business days
Standard production lead time. Factors affecting duration:
- Quantity: 500-2,000 pieces on the faster end; 10,000+ pieces on the longer end
- Complexity: Simple designs produce faster than complex ones
- Season: Peak season (August-November for holiday orders) may extend lead times
- Material availability: Custom materials may extend the timeline

Phase 9: Quality Inspection — 2-3 business days
Final random inspection using AQL 2.5 standards. If the client arranges third-party inspection, additional time must be scheduled for the inspector's visit.

Phase 10: Packing and Loading — 2-3 business days
Final packing into export cartons, palletizing, and loading into containers or arranging LCL (Less than Container Load) shipment.

Phase 11: Ocean Freight — 15-35 days (varies by destination)
Typical ocean freight times from Ningbo/Shanghai port:
- To US West Coast (Los Angeles, Long Beach): 15-20 days
- To US East Coast (New York, Savannah): 25-35 days
- To Europe (Rotterdam, Hamburg): 25-30 days
- To Southeast Asia: 7-15 days
- To Australia: 15-20 days

Phase 12: Air Freight (alternative) — 3-7 days
For urgent orders or small quantities:
- To USA: 5-7 days
- To Europe: 4-6 days
- To Southeast Asia: 2-4 days
Cost is significantly higher than ocean freight, typically $4-$8 per kg.

TOTAL TIMELINE EXAMPLES

Example 1: Standard OEM Order (US Retailer, 3,000 pieces)
- Quotation: 2 days
- Sampling: 10 days
- Sample shipping: 5 days
- Client approval: 5 days
- Material procurement: 7 days
- Production: 35 days
- QC and packing: 5 days
- Ocean freight to US West Coast: 18 days
- TOTAL: Approximately 87 days (about 3 months)

Example 2: Rush Order (1,000 pieces, simple design)
- Quotation: 1 day
- Sampling: 7 days
- Sample shipping (DHL): 4 days
- Client approval: 2 days
- Material procurement: 5 days
- Rush production: 20 days
- QC and packing: 3 days
- Air freight: 5 days
- TOTAL: Approximately 47 days (about 7 weeks)

Example 3: ODM Order with Design (new product, 5,000 pieces)
- Quotation: 2 days
- Design and concept: 8 days
- Sampling: 12 days + 1 revision round (8 days)
- Sample shipping x2: 10 days
- Client approvals: 8 days
- Material procurement: 10 days
- Production: 40 days
- QC and packing: 5 days
- Ocean freight to Europe: 28 days
- TOTAL: Approximately 131 days (about 4.5 months)

RUSH PRODUCTION

Rush production is available with a minimum lead time of 20 days for manufacturing (excluding material procurement and shipping). Rush surcharge is typically 10-20% above standard pricing. Availability depends on current factory capacity.

PLANNING RECOMMENDATIONS

- For Christmas/holiday products: Place orders by June-July
- For Valentine's Day: Place orders by October-November
- For Easter: Place orders by November-December
- Allow buffer time of 2-3 weeks for unexpected delays

LovelyJoy — 毛绒玩具生产周期与时间规划
============================================================

概述

了解生产周期对产品发布、季节性推广和零售交货窗口的规划至关重要。本文详细说明从初始询价到货物到达仓库的每个阶段。

各阶段时间

第1阶段：询价与报价 — 1-3个工作日
第2阶段：设计与概念（仅ODM）— 5-10个工作日
第3阶段：打样 — 7-15个工作日
第4阶段：样品快递 — 4-7个工作日
第5阶段：客户审核与确认 — 视客户而定（建议预留3-5天）
第6阶段：原材料采购 — 5-10个工作日
第7阶段：产前样 — 3-5个工作日
第8阶段：量产 — 30-45个工作日
第9阶段：质量检验 — 2-3个工作日
第10阶段：包装装箱 — 2-3个工作日
第11阶段：海运 — 15-35天（视目的地）
第12阶段：空运（备选）— 3-7天

海运时间参考（宁波/上海出发）

加急生产

最快20天量产（不含备料和运输），加急费通常为标准价格的10-20%，视工厂产能而定。
