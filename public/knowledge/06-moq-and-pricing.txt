LovelyJoy — MOQ, Pricing, and Payment Terms
=============================================

MINIMUM ORDER QUANTITY (MOQ)

Standard MOQ: 500 pieces per design/SKU
Trial Order MOQ: 200 pieces per design/SKU (available for new clients)

The MOQ applies per individual design or SKU, not per total order. For example, if you order 3 different plush toy designs, each design must meet the 500-piece minimum (or 200 for trial orders), resulting in a total order of at least 1,500 pieces (or 600 for trial).

WHY IS THERE A MOQ?

Plush toy manufacturing requires setup costs for each design: pattern cutting dies, embroidery programming, material sourcing in minimum bolt quantities, and production line configuration. The 500-piece MOQ ensures these fixed costs are distributed across enough units to keep per-unit pricing competitive.

CAN THE MOQ BE LOWER?

- Trial orders of 200 pieces are available for new clients testing the market or evaluating LovelyJoy as a supplier
- Very simple designs (basic shapes, single fabric, minimal features) may qualify for lower MOQs — contact sales to discuss
- Reorders of existing designs (no new setup required) may have lower minimums
- Higher per-unit pricing applies to orders below standard MOQ

PRICING FACTORS

The per-unit price of a plush toy depends on multiple variables:

1. Size: Larger toys use more material and take longer to produce. A 15cm toy costs significantly less than a 60cm toy.

2. Materials: Premium fabrics (long-pile plush, organic cotton, licensed character fabric) cost more than standard short-pile polyester plush. Special fillings (memory foam, weighted pellets) add cost.

3. Complexity: Number of fabric pieces, number of colors, construction difficulty (articulated limbs, embedded electronics, multiple accessories) all affect labor time and cost.

4. Embroidery and printing: Amount of embroidery (facial features, logos, decorative elements) and printing (heat transfer, sublimation) add to the cost.

5. Accessories: Clothing, ribbons, bows, plastic eyes vs. embroidered eyes, magnets, sound modules, LED lights — each accessory adds material and labor cost.

6. Packaging: Basic poly bag is cheapest. Custom display boxes, hang tags, retail-ready blister packs, and custom printed bags increase packaging cost.

7. Order quantity: Higher quantities result in lower per-unit costs due to economies of scale in material purchasing and production efficiency.

8. Certifications and testing: If third-party lab testing is required (ASTM F963, EN 71, etc.), testing fees are typically $500-$2,000 per SKU depending on tests required.

LovelyJoy — 起订量、定价与付款条件
=============================================

最低起订量（MOQ）

标准MOQ：500件/款/SKU
试单MOQ：200件/款/SKU（新客户可用）

MOQ按单个设计或SKU计算，非按整单总量。例如：订购3款不同毛绒玩具，每款需达到500件（或试单200件），总量至少1,500件（或试单600件）。

为什么有MOQ？

毛绒玩具生产每款设计都有固定成本：裁剪模具、绣花编程、面料最低采购量、生产线调试等。500件MOQ确保固定成本分摊到足够数量，保持单价竞争力。

MOQ能否更低？

- 新客户可以200件起试单
- 非常简单的款式（基础造型、单一面料、少量细节）可能接受更低MOQ，详询销售
- 现有款式返单（无需新开版）可能降低起订量
- 低于标准MOQ的订单单价较高

定价因素

1. 尺寸：大尺寸用料多、工时长，成本更高
2. 材料：高端面料（长毛绒、有机棉）比标准短毛涤纶绒贵
3. 复杂度：面料片数、颜色数量、工艺难度影响工时和成本
4. 绣花和印花：绣花和印花的面积与复杂度影响成本
5. 配件：服装、丝带、塑料眼vs绣花眼、磁铁、发声器、LED等
6. 包装：基本胶袋最便宜，定制盒、吊牌、零售包装增加成本
7. 数量：量大单价低，规模效应明显
8. 认证测试：第三方测试费通常$500-$2,000/SKU
