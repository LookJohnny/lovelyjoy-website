LovelyJoy — Comprehensive FAQ for Plush Toy Buyers
=====================================================

Q1: What is LovelyJoy?
A: LovelyJoy (Chinese name: 爱儿采, legal name: Yiwu Aierkai Toys Co., Ltd.) is a plush toy manufacturer founded in 2003 in Yiwu, Zhejiang, China. We have a 20,000 sqm factory, 300+ workers, and 50+ designers. We provide OEM and ODM services for plush toys.

Q2: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
A: Standard MOQ is 500 pieces per design/SKU. Trial orders for new clients start at 200 pieces per design. Very simple designs may qualify for even lower MOQs — contact us to discuss.

Q3: How much does a custom plush toy cost?
A: Pricing depends on size, materials, complexity, and quantity. Rough ranges: small plush (10-15cm) $1-3/unit, medium (20-30cm) $2.50-6/unit, large (40-60cm) $5-15/unit at 500+ pieces. Contact us with your specifications for an accurate quote.

Q4: How long does production take?
A: Standard production lead time is 30-45 days after sample approval and deposit payment. Rush production is available in as few as 20 days with a 10-20% surcharge.

Q5: How long does sampling take?
A: Initial samples take 7-15 days. Each revision round takes 5-10 days. Most projects complete sampling in 2-4 weeks.

Q6: How much do samples cost?
A: Sample cost is $50-$200 per design depending on size and complexity. This cost is deducted from your first production order, making it effectively free when you proceed to production.

Q7: Do you offer design services?
A: Yes. LovelyJoy has 50+ in-house designers. Design service is free for orders meeting MOQ (500+ pieces). We can develop your concept from a rough sketch, description, or mood board into a production-ready design.

Q8: What certifications do you have?
A: We hold 6 certifications: BSCI (social compliance), ISO 9001 (quality management), CE (European conformity), ASTM F963 (US toy safety), EN 71 (European toy safety), and GB 6675 (China toy safety).

Q9: Can you produce licensed/IP character plush toys?
A: Yes. LovelyJoy is an authorized manufacturer for licensed IPs including Sanrio Hello Kitty, Disney Paw Patrol, and Illumination Minions. We understand licensor approval processes and brand guideline compliance.

Q10: What payment methods do you accept?
A: T/T (bank wire transfer), L/C (letter of credit), and PayPal. Standard terms are 30% deposit upon order confirmation and 70% balance before shipment.

Q11: Can you ship to my country?
A: Yes. We ship worldwide. Available Incoterms: FOB Ningbo/Shanghai, CIF, and DDP (delivered to your door). We handle all export documentation.

Q12: What materials do you use?
A: Common fabrics include short plush, crystal super-soft, long plush, minky, velboa, cotton, and recycled polyester. Fillings include PP cotton, PE pellets, and memory foam. All materials comply with international safety standards.

Q13: How do you ensure quality?
A: We use a 5-stage QC process: incoming material inspection, in-process checks, assembly/finishing inspection, final AQL 2.5 random inspection, and pre-shipment inspection. Every toy passes through a metal detector.

Q14: Can I visit the factory?
A: Yes. Factory visits and audits are welcome with advance scheduling. Our showroom in Yiwu is also open for walk-in visits. Contact us to arrange a visit.

Q15: Do you sign NDAs?
A: Yes. We provide non-disclosure agreements upon request to protect your designs and business information.

Q16: Who are your major clients?
A: We work with CVS, Burlington, Kellytoy, Miniso International, and The Green Party (凯蓝), among others.

Q17: Can you produce plush toys for children under 3 years old?
A: Yes. For the under-3 age group, we use embroidered eyes and noses (no plastic small parts), ensure no detachable accessories, and comply with all applicable safety standards. Products are tested by accredited labs.

Q18: What shipping methods are available?
A: Ocean freight (FCL or LCL), air freight, express courier (DHL/FedEx/UPS), and China-Europe rail. Ocean freight is most economical for large orders; air freight or express is best for small quantities or urgent delivery.

Q19: How do I get started?
A: Email info@lovelyjoytoy.com or WhatsApp +1 (626) 586 7567 with your product idea, reference images, target size, estimated quantity, and target market. We will respond with a quotation within 1-3 business days.

Q20: What information should I include in my inquiry?
A: For the fastest and most accurate quote, provide: design/reference images, target size, material preference, estimated quantity per SKU, target market (US, EU, etc.), desired packaging, and target delivery date.

Q21: Can you help with Amazon FBA preparation?
A: Yes. We can package and label products according to Amazon FBA requirements, including FNSKU labeling, poly bagging, and carton labeling per Amazon's specifications.

Q22: Do you offer product photography?
A: Yes. We can take product photos in our studio for your website and marketplace listings at no extra charge for production orders.

Q23: What happens if quality issues are found after delivery?
A: LovelyJoy stands behind product quality. If defects are found that were present before shipment, we work with the client to resolve the issue through replacement, credit, or rework on future orders, depending on the nature and extent of the problem.

Q24: Can you add electronics to plush toys (sound, lights)?
A: Yes. We can integrate sound modules (custom recorded messages, music), LED lights, vibration motors, and other electronic features. Additional testing may be required for electronic toy compliance.

Q25: What is the difference between OEM and ODM?
A: OEM means you provide the design and we manufacture it. ODM means we design and manufacture the product based on your concept or brief. Both services are available.
