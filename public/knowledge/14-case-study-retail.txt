LovelyJoy — Case Study: Seasonal Plush Collections for US Retail
==================================================================

BACKGROUND

A major US retail chain with over 8,000 store locations needed a plush toy collection for their upcoming Valentine's Day and Easter seasonal promotions. The retailer required multiple SKUs, strict safety compliance, retail-ready packaging, and delivery to their US distribution centers within a tight timeline.

This case study is based on LovelyJoy's experience producing seasonal plush collections for large US retailers such as CVS and Burlington.

CLIENT REQUIREMENTS

- 8 different plush toy designs (4 Valentine's Day, 4 Easter)
- Sizes ranging from 15cm to 35cm
- Total quantity: 120,000 pieces across all SKUs (15,000 per SKU)
- ASTM F963 and CPSIA compliant
- Retail-ready packaging: display boxes with UPC barcodes
- Delivery to US West Coast distribution center by specific date
- Competitive pricing under $3.50 FOB per unit for the smallest SKU

CHALLENGES

1. Tight timeline: Order placed in September for Valentine's Day delivery in early January
2. Multiple SKUs: 8 different designs required parallel production management
3. Retail compliance: Specific packaging, labeling, and routing guide requirements
4. Cost pressure: Competitive unit pricing while maintaining quality standards
5. Volume: 120,000 units needed consistent quality across entire production run

LOVELYJOY'S APPROACH

Phase 1: Design and Sampling (Weeks 1-3)
LovelyJoy's design team worked simultaneously on all 8 designs. Since the retailer provided concept sketches, this was an OEM project. LovelyJoy's role was to:
- Convert 2D sketches into production-ready patterns
- Select appropriate fabrics (crystal super-soft for premium feel, short plush for cost-effective options)
- Produce 8 initial samples within 10 days
- Ship samples via DHL for client review

Phase 2: Sample Approval (Weeks 3-5)
The retailer requested minor revisions on 3 of the 8 designs:
- Adjusted facial embroidery on the Valentine's bear
- Changed ribbon color on the Easter bunny
- Increased stuffing density on the Easter lamb
Revised samples were produced in 7 days and approved on second round.

Phase 3: Material Procurement (Weeks 5-7)
- Sourced 12 different fabric colors across 3 fabric types
- Ordered custom-printed display boxes (8 designs)
- Procured all trims, labels, hang tags, and UPC stickers
- PP cotton filling ordered in bulk for cost efficiency

Phase 4: Production (Weeks 7-12)
- All 8 SKUs entered production simultaneously across multiple sewing lines
- Daily in-process quality checks by LovelyJoy's QC team
- Metal detection on 100% of units
- Weekly progress photos and reports sent to the client
- Total production completed in 35 days

Phase 5: Quality Control and Inspection (Week 12)
- AQL 2.5 final inspection on all 8 SKUs
- Client arranged SGS pre-shipment inspection — all lots passed on first inspection
- ASTM F963 and CPSIA test reports from accredited lab provided to client

Phase 6: Packaging and Shipping (Weeks 12-13)
- Products packed in retail-ready display boxes
- Packed into export cartons per retailer's routing guide specifications
- Loaded into two 40ft HC containers
- FOB Ningbo, client's freight forwarder handled ocean freight

Phase 7: Delivery
- Containers departed Ningbo port in early December
- Arrived at US West Coast port in late December
- Delivered to retailer's distribution center in early January
- Products on store shelves by mid-January, well ahead of Valentine's Day

RESULTS

- 120,000 units delivered on time and within budget
- First-pass inspection rate: 98.5% (above AQL 2.5 standard)
- All ASTM F963 and CPSIA tests passed
- Retailer placed a repeat order for the following year's seasonal collection
- Total project timeline: 13 weeks from first inquiry to goods on water

KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

1. Parallel workflow: Sampling all 8 SKUs simultaneously rather than sequentially
2. Clear communication: Weekly updates with photos kept the client informed
3. Experienced production planning: Multiple sewing lines allocated to maintain the timeline
4. Pre-approved material sources: Fabrics from trusted suppliers reduced quality risk
5. Compliance expertise: LovelyJoy's familiarity with US retail requirements avoided labeling and packaging delays

LovelyJoy — 案例分析：美国零售商季节性毛绒玩具系列
==================================================================

背景

一家拥有8,000余家门店的美国大型零售连锁需要一批情人节和复活节季节性毛绒玩具促销品。要求多个SKU、严格安全合规、零售就绪包装，并在紧张的时间窗口内送达美国配送中心。

本案例基于爱儿采为CVS、Burlington等美国大型零售商生产季节性毛绒玩具系列的实际经验。

客户需求

- 8款不同设计（4款情人节、4款复活节）
- 尺寸15-35cm
- 总量：120,000件（每款15,000件）
- 符合ASTM F963和CPSIA
- 零售就绪包装：展示盒+UPC条码
- 交付至美西配送中心

爱儿采方案

第1-3周：8款同时打样，10天内完成
第3-5周：3款修改，第二轮确认通过
第5-7周：采购12种面料颜色、定制包装盒、辅料
第7-12周：8款同步量产，多条生产线并行，35天完成
第12周：AQL 2.5终检+SGS第三方验货，全部通过
第12-13周：装入2个40尺高柜，FOB宁波发货

成果

- 120,000件准时交付，预算内完成
- 首检合格率98.5%
- 所有安全测试通过
- 零售商次年续单
- 全程13周（从询价到装船）
