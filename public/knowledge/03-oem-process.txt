LovelyJoy — OEM Plush Toy Manufacturing Process
=================================================

WHAT IS OEM?

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing) means LovelyJoy manufactures plush toys based on the client's own designs, specifications, and branding. The client provides the design; LovelyJoy handles production. All intellectual property remains with the client.

WHO USES OEM SERVICE?

- Retail chains needing private-label plush toys
- Brand owners with existing product designs
- IP licensors requiring authorized manufacturing
- Companies with in-house design teams
- Importers and distributors with established product lines

STEP-BY-STEP OEM PROCESS

Step 1: Inquiry and Quotation (1-3 days)
Client submits product details including: reference images or technical drawings, target size, material preferences, estimated quantity, target price range, and destination market. LovelyJoy's team reviews the inquiry and provides a detailed quotation within 1-3 business days.

Step 2: Design Review and Technical Confirmation (2-5 days)
Once the quotation is accepted, LovelyJoy's engineering team reviews the client's design files. This includes: pattern analysis, material selection confirmation, construction feasibility check, safety compliance review for the target market, and cost optimization suggestions if needed. A technical specification sheet is prepared and sent to the client for approval.

Step 3: Sample Development (7-15 days)
LovelyJoy produces a physical sample (also called a prototype or pre-production sample). The first sample typically takes 7-15 days depending on complexity. Sample cost ranges from $50-$200 per design depending on size and complexity. The sample cost is deducted from the first production order.

Step 4: Sample Approval and Revisions
The sample is shipped to the client for evaluation. Clients may request revisions to dimensions, colors, materials, or construction details. LovelyJoy typically provides 1-2 rounds of free revisions. Additional revision rounds may incur extra sample charges.

Step 5: Order Confirmation and Deposit (1-3 days)
After sample approval, the client confirms the production order with quantities and delivery schedule. A 30% deposit is required via T/T (bank transfer), L/C (letter of credit), or PayPal. Production scheduling begins upon receipt of deposit.

Step 6: Raw Material Procurement (5-10 days)
LovelyJoy sources fabrics, fillings, accessories, and packaging materials. For standard materials, procurement takes 5-7 days. Specialty or custom-dyed fabrics may require 10-15 days.

Step 7: Pre-Production Sample (PP Sample) (3-5 days)
Before mass production begins, a pre-production sample is made using actual production materials and methods. This PP sample is sent to the client (or photos/videos are shared) for final confirmation.

Step 8: Mass Production (30-45 days)
Standard production lead time is 30-45 days depending on quantity and complexity. Rush production is available in as few as 20 days for an additional fee. Production follows the sequence: cutting, embroidery, sewing, stuffing, finishing, quality inspection, and packaging.

Step 9: Quality Control and Inspection
LovelyJoy conducts in-line inspections during production and a final random inspection before shipment using AQL 2.5 standards. Clients may arrange third-party inspections (SGS, Bureau Veritas, Intertek, etc.) at their own cost.

Step 10: Packaging and Shipping
Products are packed according to client specifications (poly bags, display boxes, retail packaging, etc.). Standard shipping terms available: FOB Ningbo/Shanghai, CIF, or DDP. The remaining 70% payment is due before shipment.

MINIMUM ORDER QUANTITY (MOQ)

- Standard MOQ: 500 pieces per design/SKU
- Trial order MOQ: 200 pieces per design/SKU (for new clients)
- Lower MOQs may be available for simple designs — contact sales for details

LovelyJoy — OEM 毛绒玩具代工生产流程
=================================================

什么是OEM？

OEM（原始设备制造）是指爱儿采根据客户自有的设计、规格和品牌要求进行毛绒玩具生产。客户提供设计，爱儿采负责制造。所有知识产权归客户所有。

OEM适用客户

- 需要自有品牌毛绒玩具的零售连锁
- 拥有现成产品设计的品牌商
- 需要授权生产的IP持有方
- 拥有内部设计团队的公司
- 有成熟产品线的进口商和经销商

OEM流程详解

第一步：询价与报价（1-3天）
客户提交产品信息，包括：参考图片或技术图纸、目标尺寸、面料偏好、预估数量、目标价格区间、目的地市场。爱儿采团队在1-3个工作日内提供详细报价。

第二步：设计评审与技术确认（2-5天）
报价确认后，工程团队对客户设计文件进行审核，包括：版型分析、面料选定确认、工艺可行性评估、目标市场安全合规审查、以及必要的成本优化建议。编制技术规格书发送客户确认。

第三步：打样（7-15天）
制作实物样品（即打样或预生产样品），首次打样一般需7-15天，视复杂程度而定。打样费约$50-$200/款，视尺寸和复杂度而定。打样费在首单中抵扣。

第四步：样品确认与修改
样品寄送客户评估。客户可对尺寸、颜色、材料或工艺提出修改意见。通常提供1-2次免费修改，额外修改可能产生费用。

第五步：订单确认与付款（1-3天）
样品确认后，客户确认生产订单数量和交期。需支付30%定金，支持T/T（电汇）、L/C（信用证）或PayPal。收到定金后排产。

第六步：原材料采购（5-10天）
采购面料、填充物、辅料和包装材料。常规材料采购周期5-7天，特殊或定染面料可能需10-15天。

第七步：产前样（PP样）（3-5天）
量产前用实际生产材料和工艺制作产前样，发送客户（或提供照片/视频）做最终确认。

第八步：量产（30-45天）
标准生产周期30-45天，视数量和复杂度而定。加急生产最快20天，需额外费用。生产工序：裁剪→绣花→车缝→充棉→整形→质检→包装。

第九步：质量控制与验货
生产过程中进行在线检验，出货前按AQL 2.5标准进行最终随机抽检。客户可自费安排第三方验货（SGS、BV、Intertek等）。

第十步：包装与发货
按客户要求包装（胶袋、展示盒、零售包装等）。提供FOB宁波/上海、CIF或DDP等贸易条款。发货前支付余款70%。
