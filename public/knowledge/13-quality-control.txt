LovelyJoy — Quality Control Process for Plush Toys
====================================================

OVERVIEW

LovelyJoy implements a 5-stage quality control process throughout plush toy manufacturing. Quality is not checked only at the end — it is monitored at every production phase. This systematic approach ensures consistent quality and minimizes defects before goods reach the customer.

THE 5-STAGE QC PROCESS

STAGE 1: Incoming Material Inspection (IQC — Incoming Quality Control)

What is checked:
- Fabric color accuracy against approved Pantone swatches or sealed samples
- Fabric weight, pile height, and hand feel consistency
- Filling material cleanliness and resilience
- Accessory quality (eyes, noses, ribbons, tags, packaging materials)
- Thread color matching
- Zippers, buttons, and hardware functionality

How it works:
- Each incoming material batch is sampled and inspected
- Color deviations beyond acceptable tolerance are rejected
- Fabric rolls are checked for defects (holes, stains, uneven dyeing)
- Materials that pass inspection are labeled and moved to the production floor
- Failed materials are returned to suppliers or quarantined

STAGE 2: In-Process Quality Control (IPQC)

Cutting inspection:
- Pattern pieces checked against templates for accuracy
- Fabric direction and nap consistency verified
- Piece counts matched to production requirements

Embroidery inspection:
- Thread color accuracy
- Stitch density and coverage
- Alignment and centering of embroidered elements
- No loose threads or skipped stitches
- Backing trimmed cleanly

Sewing inspection:
- Seam straightness and consistency
- Stitch count per inch (SPI) within specification
- No puckering, bunching, or skipped stitches
- Correct assembly sequence (panels in right order)
- Turn-out check (no raw edges visible)

Stuffing inspection:
- Fill weight within specified tolerance (typically +/- 5%)
- Fill distribution even throughout the toy
- Firmness matches approved sample
- Closure seam secure (hand-stitched opening properly closed)

STAGE 3: Assembly and Finishing Inspection

What is checked:
- All components properly attached (eyes, noses, accessories)
- Pull test on eyes and noses: minimum 90 Newtons (EN 71) or 15 lbs (ASTM F963)
- No exposed threads, loose ends, or stray fibers
- Symmetry: left and right sides match
- Overall shape and proportions match the sealed sample
- Surface cleanliness: no stains, marks, or foreign matter
- Brush and groom: fur direction consistent, no bald spots

Metal detection:
- Every toy passes through a needle/metal detector
- Detects broken needles, pins, staples, or any metallic contamination
- Sensitivity: Ferrous metal 1.2mm, non-ferrous 1.5mm, stainless steel 2.0mm
- Any detected items cause the toy to be pulled for inspection and rework

STAGE 4: Final Random Inspection (FQC — Final Quality Control)

AQL Standards:
LovelyJoy performs final inspection using the AQL (Acceptable Quality Level) sampling system per ISO 2859-1 (ANSI/ASQ Z1.4):

- Critical defects (safety hazards): AQL 0 — Zero tolerance
- Major defects (functional/appearance issues affecting usability): AQL 2.5
- Minor defects (cosmetic issues not affecting function): AQL 4.0

Inspection procedure:
1. Finished goods are staged in the warehouse
2. Random samples are pulled based on AQL sample size tables
3. Each sampled unit is inspected against the approved sealed sample and specification sheet
4. Measurements are taken and compared to tolerances
5. Defect types are classified (critical, major, minor) and recorded
6. If the lot passes, goods are approved for shipment
7. If the lot fails, 100% inspection is conducted, defective units are reworked or replaced, and re-inspection is performed

Items checked during final inspection:
- Dimensional accuracy (size measurements within tolerance)
- Weight accuracy
- Color accuracy against sealed sample
- Embroidery/printing quality
- Seam strength (destructive testing on sample units)
- Component attachment strength (pull tests)
- Labeling correctness (care labels, hang tags, UPC codes)
- Packaging correctness and condition
- Carton marking and packing list accuracy

STAGE 5: Pre-Shipment Inspection (PSI)

This is the final check before goods leave the warehouse:
- Carton condition: No damage, proper sealing
- Carton markings correct: Shipping marks, quantities, weights
- Random carton opening: Verify contents match packing list
- Packing quality: Products properly protected, no compression damage
- Documentation check: Commercial invoice, packing list, test reports all in order

THIRD-PARTY INSPECTION

Clients are welcome to arrange independent third-party inspections at any stage. Common inspection companies:
- SGS
- Bureau Veritas (BV)
- Intertek
- TUV
- QIMA

LovelyJoy cooperates fully with third-party inspectors. Inspection scheduling and fees are the client's responsibility.

QC DOCUMENTATION

LovelyJoy provides the following QC records upon request:
- Incoming material inspection reports
- In-process inspection checklists
- Final inspection report with photos and measurements
- Metal detection log
- Test reports from accredited labs

LovelyJoy — 毛绒玩具品质控制流程
====================================================

概述

爱儿采实施五阶段品质控制流程，贯穿毛绒玩具生产全过程，确保品质稳定，最大限度减少缺陷。

五阶段品控流程

第一阶段：来料检验（IQC）
检查面料颜色、重量、手感，填充物清洁度，辅料品质，线色匹配。不合格材料退回或隔离。

第二阶段：制程检验（IPQC）
裁剪：版型精度、面料方向一致性
绣花：线色、针密度、对位、无跳针
车缝：缝线直度、针距、无起皱
充棉：重量公差±5%、分布均匀、手感与确认样一致

第三阶段：装配与整理检验
部件安装检查、拉力测试（90N/15lbs）、对称性、整体造型、表面清洁、梳毛。
金属探测：每件过针检，灵敏度：铁1.2mm、非铁1.5mm、不锈钢2.0mm。

第四阶段：最终抽检（FQC）
按ISO 2859-1 AQL抽样标准：
- 致命缺陷（安全隐患）：AQL 0，零容忍
- 主要缺陷（功能/外观问题）：AQL 2.5
- 次要缺陷（不影响功能的外观问题）：AQL 4.0
不合格批次进行100%全检、返工、复检。

第五阶段：出货前检验（PSI）
纸箱状态、唛头、随机开箱验货、包装质量、单据完整性。

第三方验货

欢迎客户安排SGS、BV、Intertek、TUV、QIMA等第三方检验，爱儿采全力配合。

品控文档

可提供：来料检验报告、制程检验记录、最终检验报告（含照片和尺寸数据）、过针记录、实验室测试报告。
