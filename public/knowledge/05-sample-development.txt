LovelyJoy — Plush Toy Sample Development Guide
=================================================

OVERVIEW

Sample development is a critical step before mass production. LovelyJoy's sample room produces physical prototypes so clients can evaluate size, shape, texture, color accuracy, and overall quality before committing to a full production order.

TYPES OF SAMPLES

1. Initial Sample / First Sample
The first physical prototype created from a client's design or concept. This is the starting point for evaluation and refinement. Timeline: 7-15 days.

2. Revised Sample
Produced after the client requests changes to the initial sample. Common revisions include size adjustments, color corrections, material changes, or construction modifications. Timeline: 5-10 days per revision round.

3. Pre-Production Sample (PP Sample)
Made using the actual production materials, trims, and methods that will be used in mass production. This is the final reference point before manufacturing begins. Timeline: 3-5 days after material procurement.

4. Shipping Sample (also called Sealed Sample or Gold Sample)
The final approved sample that serves as the production standard. Both LovelyJoy and the client keep a copy for reference during and after production. One copy remains on the production floor as the benchmark.

WHAT TO PROVIDE FOR SAMPLING

To produce an accurate sample, clients should provide as much of the following as possible:

- Design files: 2D illustrations, sketches, or reference photos (front, side, back views)
- Dimensions: Target size in centimeters or inches
- Material preferences: Fabric type (plush, velboa, minky, cotton, etc.), color (Pantone codes preferred)
- Filling: PP cotton, PE pellets, memory foam, or other
- Features: Embroidered details, printed elements, accessories (ribbons, bows, clothing)
- Safety requirements: Target market (US, EU, China, etc.) to determine applicable standards
- Packaging: Any specific packaging requirements for the sample

If a client only has a rough idea, LovelyJoy's ODM design team can develop concepts before sampling begins.

SAMPLE COSTS

- Simple designs (basic shape, minimal details): $50-$80 per sample
- Standard designs (moderate complexity, embroidered features): $80-$150 per sample
- Complex designs (multiple materials, accessories, large size): $150-$200+ per sample

Important: Sample costs are deducted from the first production order. If you place a production order, the sample fee is effectively free.

SAMPLE TIMELINE

- Initial sample: 7-15 days from design confirmation
- Each revision round: 5-10 days
- PP sample: 3-5 days (after materials are procured)
- Most projects complete sampling in 2-4 weeks total

REVISION POLICY

- 1-2 rounds of revisions are included free of charge
- Additional revision rounds may incur charges of $30-$80 per round depending on the extent of changes
- Minor adjustments (color fine-tuning, slight size change) are typically absorbed at no extra cost
- Major redesigns (completely new shape or concept) are treated as new samples

SAMPLE SHIPPING

Samples are shipped via international express courier (DHL, FedEx, UPS). Typical delivery times:
- To USA/Canada: 4-7 business days
- To Europe: 4-7 business days
- To Southeast Asia: 3-5 business days
- To Australia: 5-7 business days
- Shipping cost is borne by the client (typically $30-$60 per shipment)

TIPS FOR FASTER SAMPLE APPROVAL

1. Provide clear, detailed design files upfront to reduce back-and-forth
2. Specify Pantone color codes rather than describing colors in words
3. Send reference products if possible — a physical sample of a similar toy helps greatly
4. Consolidate feedback into one round rather than sending piecemeal revisions
5. Use video calls to review samples in real time before shipping revisions

LovelyJoy — 毛绒玩具打样开发指南
=================================================

概述

打样是量产前的关键环节。爱儿采样品室制作实物原型，让客户在确认生产订单前评估尺寸、形状、手感、颜色准确度和整体品质。

样品类型

1. 首样
根据客户设计或概念制作的首个实物原型，是评估和改进的起点。周期：7-15天。

2. 修改样
根据客户修改意见制作的样品。常见修改包括尺寸调整、颜色校正、材料变更或工艺改动。周期：每轮5-10天。

3. 产前样（PP样）
使用量产实际材料、辅料和工艺制作，是量产前的最终参考标准。周期：材料到位后3-5天。

4. 封样（确认样/金样）
最终确认的样品，作为生产标准。爱儿采和客户各保留一份，生产线上放置一份作为对照基准。

打样需提供的信息

- 设计文件：2D插画、草图或参考图片（正面、侧面、背面）
- 尺寸：目标尺寸（厘米或英寸）
- 材料偏好：面料类型（毛绒、天鹅绒、水晶超柔、棉布等），颜色（首选潘通色号）
- 填充物：PP棉、PE颗粒、记忆棉或其他
- 特征：绣花细节、印花元素、配件（丝带、蝴蝶结、服装）
- 安全要求：目标市场（美国、欧盟、中国等），以确定适用标准
- 包装：样品的特殊包装要求

如客户仅有初步想法，爱儿采ODM设计团队可先进行概念开发。

打样费用

- 简单款（基础造型，少量细节）：$50-$80/个
- 标准款（中等复杂度，有绣花）：$80-$150/个
- 复杂款（多种材料、配件、大尺寸）：$150-$200+/个

重要提示：打样费在首次生产订单中抵扣，下单即等于免费打样。

打样周期

- 首样：设计确认后7-15天
- 每轮修改：5-10天
- 产前样：3-5天（材料到位后）
- 大多数项目2-4周内完成打样

修改政策

- 免费提供1-2轮修改
- 额外修改每轮可能收取$30-$80，视变更幅度而定
- 微调（颜色微调、尺寸小幅变更）通常免费
- 重大设计变更（全新造型或概念）按新样品处理

样品快递

通过国际快递发送（DHL、FedEx、UPS）。通常运达时间：
- 美国/加拿大：4-7个工作日
- 欧洲：4-7个工作日
- 东南亚：3-5个工作日
- 澳大利亚：5-7个工作日
- 快递费由客户承担（通常$30-$60/次）
